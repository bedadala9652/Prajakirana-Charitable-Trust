Our Vision

Quality education for orphan and poor children, poverty alleviation, environment protection.


Our Mission

Working with a holistic approach where vulnerable communities are economically sustainable. Children have access to quality education and health. Youth have access to opportunities for higher education and employment. Take care of the elders who are in need and give them enough support to live their life happily.

Services

Child Welfare

Provides free education, accommodation & food to Orphan and poor children. Rescuing orphan and vulnerable children. Financial and accessories support to specially abled and handicapped children.

Senior Citizen Welfare

Provides medicines, wheelchairs, walking sticks, crutches and medical facilities to senior citizen. Organizes free health check up camps to senior citizen. Organizes recreation activities for senior citizen.

Dog care

We rescue street dogs and provide them proper aid and food,We have built a shelter for dogs.Proper vaccination and care is taken on the man's bestfriend.We encourage everyone to pay a visit to our dog shelter and play with them.

Women Empowerment

Provides vocational training to women. Organizes self defence training programme to women. Health and hygiene awareness drives for women.

Labour Welfare

Organizes safety, health and hygiene awareness programmes to labour. Provides food to migrant labours. Provides support to migrant labours children’s education. Helps to labours to get government schemes benefits.
