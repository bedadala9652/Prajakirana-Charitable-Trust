ABOUT US

Prajakirana Seva Charitable Trust was founded in the year 2019. Prajakirana Seva Charitable Trust is a non-government and not for profit organization which has been striving to provide a wide range of social services to the society. Prajakirana Seva Charitable Trust aims to emphasize a great deal of working for building the capacity, communications, opportunities and developmental approach for all stratum of society. Prajakirana Seva Charitable Trust is working in the areas of Education, Health, Environment Protection, Women Empowerment, Youth Empowerment, Skilling, Senior Citizen Welfare, Rural Development and other social issues which need immidiate attention. Our main project is “PSCT Children Home”. Here we provide free education, accommodation and food for orphans and poor children. This project aims to provide quality education and focuses on overall development of the child. Here we support and train child to achieve big in their interesting field.

OUR VISION

Quality education for orphan and poor children, poverty alleviation, environment protection.

OUR MISSION

Working with holistic approach where vulnerable communities are economically sustainable.
Children have access to quality education and health.
Youth have access to opportunities for higher education and employment.
Take care of the elders who are in need and give them enough support to live their life happily.

FOUNDER

“I lost my mother and father in my childhood. I have gone through lot of difficulties in my life. I have experienced the pain of orphans and difficulties they face in their life. These experiences in my life made me to enter into social service. I decided to dedicate my life for welfare of orphans.”                                                                                                                                         - Rahul Gali

Mr. Rahul Gali is 31 years old. He is from Mudhol of Northern Karnataka. He is a B.Com graduate.
Came to Bengaluru in the year 2013 for job purpose. He worked in film industry as casting and art director.
He worked with many social service organisations along with his job. He founded Prajakirana Seva Charitable Trust
in the year 2019. He has received several awards and acknowledgments for his social works.
